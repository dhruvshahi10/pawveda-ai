# SEO Master Plan — PawVeda

## Goal
Rank in India for high-intent pet care searches (dogs & cats first) with a scalable content engine.

## Primary GEO
India (Bengaluru, Mumbai, Pune, Delhi NCR prioritized)

## Core Pillars (4)
1) Food Safety (Indian kitchen)
2) Climate Safety (heat, AQI, monsoon)
3) Daily Care (routine, hygiene, vaccination reminders)
4) Breed Intelligence (breed-specific routines, India context)

## Content Targets
- Dogs: 20 blogs
- Cats: 20 blogs
- Each pillar: 10 supporting blogs (clusters)
- 4 pillar pages as category hubs

## Publishing Rhythm (MVP)
Week 1: Pillars + 5 dog blogs (Food Safety)
Week 2: 5 dog blogs (Climate + Daily)
Week 3: 5 cat blogs (Food Safety + Daily)
Week 4: 5 cat blogs (Climate + Breed)

## Quality Bar (non-negotiable)
Every blog must include:
- Quick Answer (40–60 words)
- India-specific guidance
- Clear safe/unsafe list
- FAQ section (3–5)
- Internal links: 1 pillar + 2 blogs
- Disclaimer block
