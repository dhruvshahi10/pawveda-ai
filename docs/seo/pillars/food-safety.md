# Food Safety for Indian Pets (Dogs & Cats)

## What this guide covers
Indian homes have unique pet risks: dal/curd habits, masalas, salt/oil, milk myths, and “one bite won’t hurt” feeding.

## Start here (fast rules)
- Avoid onion/garlic, grapes/raisins, chocolate, xylitol.
- Keep salt/oil low even in “home food”.
- Introduce new foods slowly.
